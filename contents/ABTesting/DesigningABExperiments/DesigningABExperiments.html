Sample size definition

  • determine sample size to get statistically significant results
  • look for increase sensitivity \( \left( 1-\beta \right)\), also known as statistical power
  • have enough power to conclude with high probability that results are statistically significant

statistical power quick overview

\(\alpha = P \left( \text{reject null | null true} \right)\) \(\beta = P \left( \text{fail to reject | null false} \right)\)
Small sample \(\alpha\) → low \(\beta\) → high
Small sample \(\alpha\) → same \(\beta\) → lower: power increased
