Intro to A/B testing

A/B testing

  • randomized experiment with two variants, A and B
  • control & variation group → randomly assigned
  • goal: scientifically determine how to optimize a web site or a mobile apps
  • way to compare two versions of single variable: testing subjects response to \(A\) vs \(B\)
  • a form of statistical hypothesis testing with two variants
